    Topic 2.1 - Molecules to Metabolism
    What are the type of reaction and the product(s) shown in this reaction?
    A. Reaction: Condensation | Product(s): Two Nucleotides

    B. Reaction: Condensation | Product(s): One Dinucleotide

    C. Reaction: Hydrolysis | Product(s): Two Nucleotides

    D. Reaction: Hydrolysis | Product(s): One Dinucleotide

    B

    Condensation reaction is a reaction in which two molecules combine to form a single molecule. The diagram depicts two molecules forming a single molecule, so it's condensation. Because it's a condensation reaction, a polymer is formed, hence the product is a dinucleotide.
